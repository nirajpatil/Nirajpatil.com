    • Hi, my name is Niraj Patil, and I’m an artist, designer, and creative director at Sparkart. I’ve spent over eight years designing and developing websites and digital experiences for clients like Redbox, Microsoft, America’s Cup, The Killers, and Bon Jovi. In my spare time I help Oakland's creative initiatives and non-profits find their feet.

    Right now I'm spending nights and weekends designing and developing an iOS app to help comic book creators, and learning more about the iOS ecosystem. I’m also reading Extremity Retained to learn more about the history of death metal. I have mad love for music, art, technology and creator communities.

    Most times you’ll find me trying to use Twitter more regularly, and on rare occasion, you’ll see me sketching strangers on BART. If there’s a fun project you’d like help with, or you'd just want to say Hi, contact me. I'm always here.
