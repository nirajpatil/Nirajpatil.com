    I designed the app's screens, interactions, and icons

    There's some sweet functionality in this app

    Issues lets users:

    1. Recommend comics to friends through sharing.

    2. Save comics to default lists: Want and Have.

    3. Build collections by creating new lists. Lists are shared with users, in and outside of the app.

    Tagging comics

    In Issues, users tag comics with words and phrases that describe them well. Other users swipe these 'tags', voting them up or down. This keeps tags relevant to the issue and makes it easier for new readers to find comics of interest quickly.

    Issues-app.com lets Issues users share their favorite comics with non-Issues users. Comics are represented by unique cards (show below) and are shared via unique URLs.
