    • Hey Bub,

    I’m working on a cool iOS app called Issues that helps readers keep track of their comic books collections.

    Issues is awesome because:

    1. It allows users to create and organize comic book collections more efficiently.

    2. It recommends comic books to friends and family by helping them search through comic book titles and (user-generated) tags.

    3. It brings fans and creators closer together, giving writers, artists and publishers more love for their work.

    4. It helps forgetful bastards like myself create unlimited Best Of collections.

    Issues app screens and icons

    Using Issues

    1. Users recommend issues via iOS’s ‘Share To’ dialog.

    2. Users save issues to two lists: Want, and Have.

    3. Users build collections by creating new lists. These lists are shared with users, in and outside of the app.

    Issues display a number of user-created tags. Tags help users find comics quicker through Search.

    Within the issue, users swipe tags to vote them up or down. This helps makes tags more or less relevant to the issue, making it easier for other users to find comics by specific interests quicker.

    Issues-app.com

    Outside of the app, I’ve designed a simple site that helps users share interesting titles with friends, and asks them to join the Issues community by downloading the app.
