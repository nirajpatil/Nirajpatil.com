    • Building a community of makers.

    Man Made It was more than an online retail store. They built a library of stories rich in creation and process; stories to be shared with makers, hobbyists, and buyers around the world. Man Made It sold unique, hand-crafted products born out of studios, workshops and garages of talented, independant makers.

    I worked with Man Made It to design and develop a unique store platform that allowed makers to tell stories about their craft while simultaneously selling their products. I helped the Man Made It team define their messaging, build their brand, design their site, and develop core modulues for core development.
