Sparkart's a strange beast to describe. My time with the digital agency over the past couple of years has seen immense growth, countless experiments, great successes and the occasional failures. Run by creative individuals with technology mindsets and startup lifestyles, the agency boasts a hybrid character whose energy I've turned into a colorful universe of objects and patterns.

Colors represent Sparkart's infectious culture; the patterns reflect its experimental nature, and the objects describe the many unique experiences Sparkart has created for millions of users over the past decade. There's never a dull day at Sparkart, and the site design conveys that message clearly. In Summer 2013, the site will grow to showcase agency work, and you'll see more universe concepts in action.

I'm a user experience designer leading a team of talented individuals at Sparkart. I live in Oakland, CA, home of Neurosis and the whistle tip. I eat a lot, run a little and listen to a lot of heavy metal. Let's hang out!
